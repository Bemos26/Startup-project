who are we?

Over the past years, we have seen students, parents and teachers struggle to connect. Students often do not have a platform to learn from. Teachers on the other hand do not have a platform to showcase what they have got and parents are unable to easily monitor their children's progress. As EduRahisi therefore, we have seen it prudent to provide a 3 in 1 platform where all these things can happen. EduRahisi is more than just a platform - it is a community dedicated to making education more inclusive, engaging and effective; shaping the mentality of learners and the future of learning

"When commitment meets smartwork, even the smallest of the visions can be brought to life" ~ Benson Mose - Founder, EduRahisi

What do we offer?

  • For Students

    Access to a wide range of learning materials and resources. engaging lessons in math, coding and languages.

  • For Teachers

    A platform to share knowledge and connect with students. A dashboard to track student progress and engagement.

  • For Parents

    Tools to monitor and support their children's education. A dashboard to view student progress and engagement.
